Our Story

Our Founder, Chad Quilling, was working away for his CS 260 class at BYU when he had the idea, 'What I create a website about a fake business that sells placeholder images?'. That is how Ipsum Designs was born. Since September of 2021, we have been serving the purpose of a practice website for Chad and have been allowing him to get good grades. All thanks to our design, and our motto, "Why write copy, when you can copy copy."
